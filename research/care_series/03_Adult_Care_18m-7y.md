# Adult Care (18 months-7 years)

> [!summary]
> The adult stage emphasizes prevention: steady body condition, oral care, parasite prevention, and annual risk review.

> [!warning]
> If your dog shows new fatigue, exercise intolerance, collapse, or persistent cough, do not delay veterinary assessment.

## Maintenance Routine
| Routine | Owner Action | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Body condition | Score and adjust portions | Weekly |
| Dental care | Home oral routine + professional planning | Daily + annual |
| Parasite control | Follow regional protocol | Year-round |
| Enrichment | Mix physical and cognitive activities | Daily |

## Weight and Mobility
- Aim for a stable healthy condition score.
- Use low-impact conditioning if stiffness appears.

## Preventive Vet Review
- Update vaccine and parasite strategy based on lifestyle and travel.
- Bring a symptom and behavior log to annual visits.
